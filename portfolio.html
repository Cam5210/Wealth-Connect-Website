Regional RIA

Accelerated Growth Through Strategic Recruiting

Business Development Recruiting
Challenge

A regional RIA with $2B AUM was struggling to attract experienced advisors despite a competitive payout and strong platform. Their internal recruiting efforts had plateaued.

Solution

Wealth Connect 360 engaged our recruiting partner to develop a targeted outreach strategy, leveraging our network of advisor relationships to identify and approach candidates who were a strong cultural fit.

Outcome

6 experienced advisors recruited within 12 months, adding $400M in new AUM and significantly expanding the firm's presence in two new markets.

FinTech Vendor

Breaking Into the Retirement Plan Market

Fractional Sales Market Entry
Challenge

An emerging fintech company had developed a powerful participant engagement tool but lacked the relationships and credibility to get meetings with major recordkeepers and plan sponsors.

Solution

Wealth Connect 360 served as their fractional sales team, leveraging our existing relationships to secure introductions and participating actively in the sales process to help position the solution.

Outcome

3 pilot programs secured with mid-size recordkeepers within 8 months, leading to 2 enterprise contracts and a $5M Series A investment.

Asset Manager

Revitalizing a Stalled Distribution Effort

Marketing Demand Gen
Challenge

A boutique asset manager had excellent products but was struggling to generate awareness and leads in a crowded market. Their small internal team couldn't compete with larger firms' marketing budgets.

Solution

Our marketing partner developed a targeted content and email campaign focused on the manager's differentiated investment approach, reaching decision-makers at RIAs and family offices.

Outcome

150+ qualified meetings generated over 6 months, resulting in $75M in new institutional mandates and a sustainable pipeline of opportunities.
