Our Story

Built on Relationships, Driven by Results

Wealth Connect was created from a simple observation: too many great solutions struggle to reach the firms that need them most.

After decades of building relationships across the wealth management and retirement industry, we saw an opportunity to do something different. Instead of firms going it alone—each hiring their own sales teams, building their own networks, fighting for the same attention—what if they could come together?

That's the idea behind Wealth Connect: a curated collective of specialized providers who share a go-to-market engine. We combine our relationships, our expertise, and our market access to help every firm in the portfolio succeed.

"We don't just open doors—we walk through them with our partners and help close the deal."

— Michele, Founder
